About Us

We Built What We Wished Existed.

The Oz Indie Collective was founded by a group of Australian musicians, engineers and creative professionals who were tired of working in isolation — and tired of the industry's gatekeeping. We decided to build the community we always wanted.

Our Belief
"Australia's creative industry is full of extraordinary talent. What it lacks is infrastructure — the networks, resources and community that make careers possible. We're building that infrastructure."

Why We Vet Everyone

Most creative platforms are open to anyone. That means spam, fake profiles, time-wasters and bad actors — noise that drowns out genuine connection.

We personally review every single application. Not because we want to be exclusive — but because we want every person you connect with here to be real, genuine and worthy of your time.

Vetting isn't gatekeeping. It's curation. It's quality control. It's how we make sure the Collective stays valuable for everyone in it.
