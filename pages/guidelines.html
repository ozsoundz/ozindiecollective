Community Guidelines

How We Treat Each Other

Last updated: July 2025. These guidelines apply to all members of the Oz Indie Collective across all platforms and interactions.

The short version: Be a genuine, respectful professional. Support your fellow creatives. Don't be a dickhead. Everything else flows from that.

1. Be Real

Every member of the Oz Indie Collective has been personally vetted. That trust comes with responsibility. Your profile must accurately represent who you are and what you do.

  • Use your real name and a genuine profile photo
  • Accurately describe your skills, experience and availability
  • Don't misrepresent your credits or portfolio
  • Keep your profile up to date

2. Be Professional

The Collective is a professional community. Treat every interaction — in the community feed, direct messages, at events and in collaborations — with the professionalism you'd expect from others.

  • Respond to messages and enquiries in a timely manner
  • Be clear and upfront about your rates, availability and expectations
  • Honour your commitments and communicate proactively if things change
  • Provide constructive, honest feedback when asked

3. Support the Community

The strength of the Collective comes from members genuinely helping each other. Share knowledge, celebrate others' wins and pay it forward.

  • Share resources, opportunities and industry information generously
  • Celebrate and amplify the achievements of fellow members
  • Offer help when you can — even when there's nothing in it for you
  • Refer and recommend others honestly and specifically

4. Post with Purpose

The community feed is for genuine creative industry conversation — not self-promotion spam or low-effort content.

  • Share work, wins, questions, resources and opportunities
  • Keep promotional posts to a reasonable frequency
  • Don't post the same content repeatedly
  • Engage with others' posts — it's a community, not a billboard

5. Zero Tolerance

The following will result in immediate and permanent removal from the Collective, with no warning and no refund of any membership fees:

  • Harassment, bullying or intimidation of any member
  • Discrimination on the basis of race, gender, sexuality, disability, age or religion
  • Sharing another member's private information without consent
  • Scamming, fraud or misrepresentation for financial gain
  • Posting spam, pyramid schemes or unsolicited commercial messages
  • Creating a fake profile or impersonating another person
  • Sexual harassment of any kind, in any context

6. Disputes & Conflicts

Creative collaborations sometimes go wrong. If you have a dispute with another member, we encourage you to resolve it directly and professionally first. If that's not possible, contact us at hello@ozindiecollective.com.au and we'll help mediate.

Don't air disputes publicly in the community feed. Private conflicts should stay private until resolved.

7. Reporting

If you see behaviour that violates these guidelines, please report it. Use the report button on any post or profile, or email us directly. All reports are handled confidentially.

We take every report seriously and respond within 48 hours.

8. Consequences

Depending on the severity of the breach, consequences range from a private warning through to temporary suspension and permanent removal. Serious violations — particularly those in section 5 — go straight to permanent removal.

We don't issue warnings for serious harm. We act immediately to protect the community.

✓These guidelines reflect the community we're building together. If you see them being upheld well — or not well enough — we want to hear from you. Get in touch.
